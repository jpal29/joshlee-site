Expected troop loss in a risk attack

Calculator to estimate number of troops lost in attack and defense in a turn of risk. I had lost a few rounds of risk and was later shown a reddit post that had a probability calculator of whether or not a Risk attack would fail (or succeed). Reading it made me wonder if there could be a way to also estimate the number of troops lost in a given attack or defence. There had been several times when I probably wouldn't have launched an attack if I had known how many troops would be lost in the process.

Since the code in the reddit post already used Monte Carlo simulation to determine the probability of winning or losing the attack, I simply had to add a little something to keep track of the troops lost during the attack. Then, it was just as simple as sticking that code inside a Shiny App that could display the expected troops remaining. Finished product in case you need it.
